About Beak n Whiskers

Beak n Whiskers, a Pet Care Management System designed to simplify and streamline the care of your beloved pets. Whether it’s managing health records, keeping track of appointments, or supporting your pet’s overall well-being, Beak n Whiskers makes pet care easier and more efficient. This app was developed by Team DevPulse, under the guidance of Professor Mohtishim, with the goal of enhancing the experience of pet care for both pet owners and professionals alike.
